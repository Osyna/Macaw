What Macaw does

Everything you need to talk to your computer

Simple on the outside, powerful underneath. Pick a model, hit your hotkey, and go.

Choose your model

Two dozen models across seven engines — Whisper, Moonshine, Parakeet, Canary-Qwen, Voxtral, sherpa-onnx, and opt-in GPT-4o cloud. Trade a little speed for accuracy, or the other way around — your call.

Types for you

Just talk and your words land straight in the window you're using. Turn on live typing to watch them appear as you speak.

Or copy & paste

Prefer to place text yourself? Macaw can drop your words on the clipboard, ready to paste wherever you like.

Any mic, any language

Pick your microphone and speak your language. Macaw understands dozens, from English and French to Japanese and beyond.

Smart punctuation

Periods, commas and question marks land where they should, so your text reads clean and is ready to send right away.

Knows when you stop

Set how long Macaw waits after you go quiet before wrapping up the sentence. Pause to think as long as you need.

Bring your own brain

24 speech models, seven engines

Swap models any time from Settings. Each one is open and downloads straight from its makers — here's who builds them.

Whisper

99+ langs

OpenAI's Whisper, sped up with CTranslate2 by SYSTRAN. Ten sizes from tiny to large-v3-turbo, plus distilled English builds.

tiny base small medium large-v3 large-v3-turbo +4 distil
SYSTRAN/faster-whisper

Moonshine

English

Tiny, ultra-fast English models from Useful Sensors, built for real-time speech on modest hardware.

moonshine/tiny moonshine/base
usefulsensors/moonshine

Parakeet

25 langs

NVIDIA's fast, accurate ASR from the NeMo toolkit. v3 adds 25 European languages. Great on GPU.

parakeet-tdt-0.6b-v2 parakeet-tdt-0.6b-v3
NVIDIA/NeMo

Canary-Qwen

English

NVIDIA's speech-LLM hybrid — a Canary encoder paired with a Qwen language model for top-tier English accuracy.

canary-qwen-2.5b
NVIDIA/NeMo

Voxtral

13 langs

Mistral AI's speech model, running through Hugging Face Transformers. Multilingual and strong on longer audio.

Voxtral-Mini-3B
mistralai/Voxtral-Mini-3B

sherpa-onnx

EN · ZH · 25

Featherweight ONNX models from k2-fsa that fly on plain CPUs — including real-time streaming and bilingual Chinese-English.

parakeet-onnx v2/v3 zipformer paraformer +2 streaming
k2-fsa/sherpa-onnx

GPT-4o cloud

99+ langs

The one exception to "local": OpenAI's transcribe models, strictly opt-in. Bring your own API key — nothing leaves your machine unless you pick them.

gpt-4o-transcribe gpt-4o-mini-transcribe
OpenAI speech-to-text

Add your own

Drop a YAML file and a small backend to plug in any model. The framework handles the rest.

Get Macaw

Up and running in one line

The install script sets up Macaw on most Linux distros, with GPU support when you have it. Prefer something else? Grab a portable AppImage or install from the AUR.

Install script AppImage AUR uv / pipx
terminal 
$ curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/
  Osyna/Macaw/main/install.sh | bash

# then just run
$ macaw
